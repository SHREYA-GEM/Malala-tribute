Young Malala Got Shot

Malala Yousafzai was shot by the Taliban in 2012 for advocating girls' education. Her survival and subsequent global recognition made her a symbol of courage and resilience in the face of adversity.

MALALA FUND

The Malala Fund is a nonprofit organization founded by Malala Yousafzai that aims to promote girls' education and empower young women worldwide. Through strategic partnerships and initiatives, the fund works to break down barriers to education, provide resources and support to girls in marginalized communities, and advocate for policy changes to ensure every girl has access to quality education. Their work continues to make a significant impact in transforming the lives of girls and promoting equality.
